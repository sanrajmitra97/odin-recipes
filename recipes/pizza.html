Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Today we shall be learning how to make the Pepperoni Pizza. The Pepperoni Pizza is my favourite dish, and it narrowly beats the American Cheese Burger. To make the Pepperoni Pizza, we shall be requiring the following ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 18 to 20 slices of Pepperoni
    • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
