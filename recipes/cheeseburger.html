Cheese Burger Recipe

Description

I love eating a cheeseburger. The combination of the cheese, patty and pickle makes it taste amazing. Kind of reminds me of the movie "Menu" :O, or the "Pink Panther". Let's learn how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese Slices
  • Pickle
  • Letture
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Sear the beef patty till satisfactory "doneness". I personally like medium.
  2. Add in the cheese slice to let the cheese melt slowly and gradually
  3. Add in the rest of the ingredients as you deem fit
  4. And voila, we now have one juicy tasty burger
