Aglio Olio Pasta Recipe

Description

Probably my favourite pasta type, I just love the olive oil used and the shrimps and the garlic...MMM! Let us look at the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Different Seasonings
  • Sausages/Hotdogs (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Add oil to the pan
  3. Add garlic and the sausages
  4. At the same time, boil the pasta
  5. Once pasta is boiled, add pasta to the pan with garlic and sausages and mix. You can then add the tomato sauce
  6. Stir everything till smells yummy!
  7. Add in seasoning
  8. The end! Enjoy
